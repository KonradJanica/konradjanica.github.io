The easiest way to fling through algorithms

MOBILE APP DESIGN

  • Fling through Infinitely
    Fling through a constantly parsing question list. Allows answers on demand via the built in webkit browser.
  • Save Favorites
    Questions can be favorited at anytime, allowing for connectionless algorithm practice on previously saved cards.
  • Apply Filters
    Filter through your favorite companies and/or that position you always wanted. Questions are from real interviews around the world.

FEATURE RICH

aMatch is the best way to practice your algorithm skills and get that job at Google with endless technical questions on your iOS or Android device. Using Career Cup to fetch the latest interview problems, users are able to easily see recently asked questions for their favorite company and desired position.
